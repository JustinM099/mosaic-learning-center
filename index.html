Montessori Method

The Montessori method is an educational philosophy developed by Dr. Maria Montessori. Her basic principle was to “follow the child”. The Montessori method emphasizes independence, freedom with limits, and respect for the child. A Montessori classroom is carefully prepared to allow the child to work independently and allow for the joy of self-discovery.

RIE Philosophy

The RIE philosophy was developed by Magda Gerber. RIE stands for Resources of Infant Educators. This philosophy believes that parents and caretakers must have respect for children, and must trust that children are natural inventors, explorers, and self-learners. Respect is the cornerstone of the RIE philosophy.

Our Philosophy

Mosaic Learning Center's philosophy is to guide each child, help them grow, and follow their natural curiosity while giving them the opportunity to learn. We respect each child's voice and will listen to what they have to say while answering their questions and helping discover the answers together. We believe that the way that we live and exist on the planet is important. We believe in getting outside and exploring the world while working hard to reduce our footprint and the waste that we make. We promote love for the Earth, animals, and our fellow humans.
